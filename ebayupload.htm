File Submission Process
Once you are sure you have prepared your file according to our FAQ, the next step will be to upload your print ready files.
WE REQUIRE TWO FLAT DESIGN FILES (FRONT/BACK), THEY MUST BE IN CMYK AT 300 DPI TO BE ACCEPTED. WE SUPPORT JPG AND PDF FORMATS.
PLEASE USE A COMPRESSION UTILITY SUCH AS STUFFIT DELUXE (http://www.aladdinsys.com) OR WINZIP (http://www.winzip.com) TO COMPRESS YOUR JOB FOLDER FOR SUBMISSION.

You can upload your files to our servers conveniently through our website. Simply browse for your file then click on the "Send File!" button.

Be sure to name your file with your eBay Username and Date (MMDDYYYY)!.
